You can now run Loc Validation through Unreal Engine!

Step 1
Go to Edit->Plugins and find the Project->Tools->Localization Validator plugin
(no, its not under 'Built-In->Localization' unfortunately)
Enable (and possibly restart the Editor)

Step 2
Click Localization->Validate Localization

Step 3
Evaluate the errors, fix the loc keys and repeat!
